# DAVE'S STRONG SYSTEM
## Complete Studio Training System for Adults 50+
### Semi-Private Training Program

---

# SECTION 1: THE BASICS

## Core Philosophy
- **Goal:** Stronger, Leaner, More Mobile, Pain-Free
- **Demographic:** Adults 50+ with orthopedic considerations
- **Format:** Semi-private groups (up to 4), 3 sessions/week

## The Big 7 Movement Patterns
Every session includes:
1. **Squat/Pull** (lower body + back)
2. **Chest/Hamstring** (push + posterior chain)
3. **Shoulder/Glute** (vertical push + hip health)
4. **Rotation/Gait** (mobility + functional carry)

---

# SECTION 2: THE STRUCTURE

## Each Session Has 4 Sections

| Section | Focus | Repetition Scheme |
|---------|-------|-------------------|
| **A** | Primary Movement | 8/5/3 Protocol (intensity) |
| **B** | Secondary Movement | 3 × 10 reps (volume) |
| **C** | Accessory Movement | 3 × 15 reps (endurance) |
| **D** | Rotation/Gait | 3 × Max reps (capacity) |

## Rotation Schedule

| Day | A (8/5/3) | B | C | D |
|-----|---------|---|---|---|
| **Session A** | Squat/Pull | Chest/Hamstring | Shoulder/Glute | Rotation/Gait |
| **Session B** | Chest/Hamstring | Shoulder/Glute | Squat/Pull | Rotation/Gait |
| **Session C** | Shoulder/Glute | Squat/Pull | Chest/Hamstring | Rotation/Gait |

**Why rotate?** Prevents preferential treatment — the "freshest" work goes to the first section each day.

# SECTION 3: THE 8/5/3 PROTOCOL (Section A)

## The 8/5/3 Progression

| Week | Set 1 | Set 2 | Set 3 (AMRAP) | Minimum to Progress |
|------|-------|-------|---------------|---------------------|
| **1** | 50% × 8 | 60% × 8 | **70%** × 8 | 8 reps |
| **2** | 65% × 5 | 75% × 5 | **85%** × 5 | 5 reps |
| **3** | 70% × 3 | 80% × 3 | **90%** × 3 | 3 reps |
| **4** | 50% × 5 | 50% × 5 | 50% × 5 | Deload |

## 8/5/3 Decision Tree

```
WEEK 4 (Deload)
   ↓
WEEK 1: Hit 8+ reps on Set 3? ──NO──→ Repeat Week 1 at same weight
   ↓ YES
WEEK 2: Hit 5+ reps on Set 3? ──NO──→ Repeat Week 1 at same weight
   ↓ YES
WEEK 3: Hit 3+ reps on Set 3? ──NO──→ Repeat Week 1 at same weight
   ↓ YES
WEEK 4: Deload as prescribed
   ↓
NEXT CYCLE: Add 5-10 lbs to Training Max, restart Week 1
```

## Training Max (TM) Selection

**For new clients or detrained 50+:**
- Start with **80% of true 1-rep max**
- This accounts for recovery and keeps them in the game

**Example:**
- True max: 100 lbs → Training Max: 80 lbs
- Week 1 Set 3: 80 × 0.70 = 56 lbs

# SECTION 4: SECTIONS B, C, D

## Section B: 3 × 10 (Volume)

**Decision Tree:**
```
Set 3: Go for max reps (STOP AT 20)
   ↓
Hit 15+ reps? ──YES──→ INCREASE weight next week
   ↓ NO
Under 15 reps? ──YES──→ STAY or DECREASE weight
```
*Why stop at 20? You get the info you need. They don't need to go higher.*

## Section C: 3 × 15 (Endurance)

**Decision Tree:**
```
Set 3: Go for max reps (STOP AT 20)
   ↓
Hit 15+ reps? ──YES──→ INCREASE weight next week
   ↓ NO
Under 15 reps? ──YES──→ STAY or DECREASE weight
```
*Same logic as Section B — stop at 20, if they hit 15+ increase.*

## Section D: Rotation/Gait (Capacity)

**Decision Tree:**
```
Set 3: Max reps (walk/carry)
   ↓
Target distance/reps hit? ──YES──→ INCREASE weight or distance
   ↓ NO
Same as last week? ──NO──→ STAY at same weight
   ↓
Significantly less? ──YES──→ DECREASE weight
```

# SECTION 7: DEMOGRAPHIC MODIFICATIONS

## For Detrained/Deconditioned

| Change | How To |
|--------|--------|
| Reduce from 3 to 2 sets | Sections B and C only |
| Extend rest periods | 90-120 seconds instead of 60 |
| Start with body weight only | Master movement before load |
| More regressions available | Use tier system from previous systems |

## For Orthopedic Issues

| Issue | Use Instead Of |
|-------|----------------|
| Shoulder problems | Floor press, push-ups, incline |
| Lower back | Goblet squat, step-ups, leg press |
| Knee problems | Box squat, step-ups, reverse lunges |
| Hip issues | Hip thrust, glute bridges, band walks |

# SECTION 9: NO-PAIN PROTOCOL

## Pre-Session Check

Ask: "How does [body part] feel today? (0-10)"
- 0-3: Proceed normally
- 4-6: Reduce load 20%, watch form
- 7+: Skip exercise, substitute

## During Session
**3-Rep Rule:** If something feels off for 3 consecutive reps, STOP. Film it. Send to coach.

## Post-Session
- Can you get up off the floor without using hands?
- Any new pain since starting?
- Did anything feel "off" that wasn't painful?

# SECTION 10: IMPLEMENTATION

## Starting a New Client

1. **Assessment** — Determine training max for each 8/5/3 movement
2. **Tier placement** — Foundation/Development/Performance
3. **Select exercises** — From the options list based on their body
4. **Set expectations** — Explain the decision tree, have them track
5. **Week 1** — Focus on technique, start at conservative weights

## Coaching Cues for Semi-Private

- "Section A, we're on safety bar, Week 1 numbers"
- "Section B, bench press, 3×10, last set is max reps"
- "Check in — how does that shoulder feel?"
- "Rest 60, begin in 3, 2, 1..."

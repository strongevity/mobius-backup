## NEXTDOOOR BEST PRACTICES:

1. **Keep posts short and conversational** - Write like you're talking to a neighbor
2. **Engage with every comment** - Reply to all comments within an hour
3. **Ask questions** - End posts with questions to drive engagement
4. **Use local hashtags** - #MurrellsInlet #GardenCity #SurfsideBeach
5. **Post consistently** - 5 posts per week is a good cadence
6. **Share real stories** - Customer successes (when you have them)
7. **Be helpful, not salesy** - Provide value first, sell second

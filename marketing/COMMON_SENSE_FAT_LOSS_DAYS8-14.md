## DAY 9: HYDRATION

### Morning
**Post 81:**
> 💧 HYDRATION MYSTERY SOLVED
> 
> Water doesn't "flush fat."
> 
> But...
> 
> • It helps control appetite
> • It supports metabolism
> • It prevents false hunger
> 
> Drink up.

**Post 82:**
> 📊 HOW MUCH WATER:
> 
> Aim for 3-4 liters daily.
> 
> More if you exercise.
> 
> More if you're in a hot climate.
> 
> Don't guess. Measure.

**Post 83:**
> 💧 HYDRATION HACKS:
> 
> • Add lemon or cucumber
> • Set hourly reminders
> • Drink 16oz upon waking
> • Track with an app
> 
> Pick one. Do it.

### Midday
**Post 84:**
> ⚠️ DEHYDRATION SIGNS:
> 
> • Headache
> • Fatigue
> • Dark urine
> • Dizziness
> 
> If you feel any of these... drink more water.

**Post 85:**
> ☕ CAFFEINE + FAT LOSS:
> 
> Coffee is fine.
> 
> But don't overdo it.
> 
> Too much caffeine = stress = cortisol = fat storage.
> 
> Limit to 2 cups daily.

**Post 86:**
> 🥤 ELECTROLYTES MATTER:
> 
> If you're sweating a lot (working out), add electrolytes.
> 
> Sodium. Potassium. Magnesium.
> 
> Don't just drink water. Balance it.

### Evening
**Post 87:**

**Post 88:**

> Clear = you're good.

**Post 89:**
> 💧 THE WATER MYTH:
> 
> "I don't need to count calories, I just drink water"

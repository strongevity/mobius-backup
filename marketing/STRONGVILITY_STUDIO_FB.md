## DAY 6: NUTRITION BASICS

### Morning
**Post 51:**
> 🥗 YOU CAN'T OUT-TRAIN BAD NUTRITION
> 
> But you also don't need to be perfect.
> 
> Here's the simple truth 👇

**Post 52:**
> 🥩 THE #1 NUTRITION RULE:
> 
> Eat protein at every meal.
> 
> Why?
> 
> • It keeps you full
> • It preserves muscle
> • It supports recovery
> 
> Chicken, eggs, beef, fish, greek yogurt.
> 
> That's the foundation.

**Post 53:**
> 💡 PROTEIN MATH:
> 
> 4oz chicken = ~35g protein
> 
> 3 eggs = ~18g protein
> 
> 1 cup greek yogurt = ~20g protein
> 
> Add these up. That's how you eat.

### Midday
**Post 54:**
> 🧊 THE HYDRATION RULE:
> 
> 3-4 liters of water per day.
> 
> More if you exercise.
> 
> More if it's hot.
> 
> Don't guess. Measure.

**Post 55:**
> 🥑 DON'T FEAR FAT:
> 
> Healthy fats are your friend.
> 
> Avocados, nuts, olive oil.
>

**Post 56:**
